# Refund Policy

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied.

## Eligibility

- Product must be in original condition
- Digital products: No refund after download (except technical issues)
- Services: Pro-rated refund for unused portion

## Process

1. Contact support with order number
2. Provide reason for refund (optional, helps us improve)
3. Return shipping label provided (if applicable)
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

## Return Shipping

- Free return shipping for defective items
- Customer pays return shipping for change of mind
- Return shipping cost deducted from refund

## Exceptions

- Sale items: Store credit only
- Custom/personalized items: No refunds
- Gift cards: Non-refundable
