# Shipping Information

## Delivery Times

### Domestic (US)
- Standard: 3-5 business days
- Express: 1-2 business days
- Overnight: Next business day

### International
- Canada: 5-7 business days
- Europe: 7-10 business days
- Rest of world: 10-15 business days

## Shipping Costs

- Orders over $50: Free standard shipping
- Orders under $50: $5.99 standard shipping
- Express: $14.99
- Overnight: $24.99

## Tracking

- Tracking number emailed within 24 hours of shipment
- Track at: https://tracking.example.com

## Issues

### Package Lost
- Contact us if not received within timeframe
- We'll file claim with carrier
- Replacement or refund issued

### Wrong Address
- Contact us immediately if you entered wrong address
- If not shipped yet, we can update it
- If already shipped, redirection fee may apply

### Damaged in Transit
- Take photos of damage
- Contact support within 48 hours
- We'll send replacement or process refund
